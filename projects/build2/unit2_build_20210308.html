Mental Health in Tech

Survey by Open Sourcing Mental Illness, LTD circa 2014

Whether a job is good or not doesn't stop with salary or number of hours worked. Enjoying the work we do is important as well. So are fringe benefits like vacation, bonuses, and the topic of this article, health benefits.

Benefits packages generally include an array of services and features. This allows employees access to healthcare when they need it at a reasonable cost. As time goes on, we are seeing more and more companies choose benefits packages with mental health care explicitly included, which is a good thing. Because we cannot explore all benefits, lets take a look at mental health in the tech industry.
